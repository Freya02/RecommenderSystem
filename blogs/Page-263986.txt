OPPO F9 Pro Starry Purple color variant goes on sale in India



OPPO launched F9 Pro smartphone in India last month. Sunrise Red and Twilight Blue versions went on sale on August 31st. Today the Starry Purple is available in the country joining the other two color variants.


To remind you, the F9 Pro has a 6.3-inch Full HD+ display with Waterdrop Screen with screen-to-body ratio up to 90.8% for a more immersive experience, is powered by Octa-Core MediaTek Helio P60 12nm processor with up to 6GB RAM, ColorOS 5.2 based on Android 8.1 (Oreo), 16MP rear camera with LED flash, f/1.85 aperture, secondary 2MP camera and a 25MP front-facing camera, f/2.0 aperture, AI, Sony IMX576 sensor.


It has dedicated dual SIM slots, Fingerprint sensor on the back and a 3500mAh battery with VOOC Flash charge fast charging that promises 2 hours of talk time with 5-minute charge. It has a gradient color design, again a first in the F series. This uses advanced color processes. To achieve a unified design, F9 Pro for the first time has used a gradient design on the side frame, making the sides and the back a naturally connected whole.


The OPPO F9 Pro is priced at Rs. 23,990 is available from Flipkart, Amazon.in as well as offline stores.


Commenting on the same, Will Yang, Brand Director, OPPO India said:


As a brand, we like to focus on the needs of the youth and introduce technology and designs that are appreciated by the youth. After the launch of F9 Pro, we are delighted to start the first sale of the much awaited F9 Pro Starry Purple edition. With limited colors being offered in the market, we felt the color design aesthetics of the OPPO F9 Pro Starry Purple would be the perfect choice for our consumers. With every new device, we look forward to create an excellent smartphone experience for our consumers.
