OPPO F9 Pro with 6.3-inch Full HD+ display, 6GB RAM, 25MP AI front camera, dual rear cameras launched in India for Rs. 23990



OPPO has launched F9 Pro, the company’s latest mid-range smartphone in the ‘F series’ in India, as it had promised. It has a 6.3-inch Full HD+ display with Waterdrop Screen with screen-to-body ratio up to 90.8% for a more immersive experience, is powered by Octa-Core MediaTek Helio P60 12nm processor with up to 6GB RAM, ColorOS 5.2 based on Android 8.1 (Oreo), 16MP rear camera with LED flash, f/1.85 aperture, secondary 2MP camera and a 25MP front-facing camera, f/2.0 aperture, AI, Sony IMX576 sensor.


It has dedicated dual SIM slots, Fingerprint sensor on the back and a 3500mAh battery with VOOC Flash charge fast charging that promises 2 hours of talk time with 5-minute charge. It has a gradient color design, again a first in the F series. This uses advanced color processes. To achieve a unified design, F9 Pro for the first time has used a gradient design on the side frame, making the sides and the back a naturally connected whole.


OPPO F9 Pro specifications

  * 6.3-inch (2280 x 1080 pixels) Full HD+ 19.5:9 aspect ratio display
  * Octa Core MediaTek Helio P60 12nm processor (Quad 2GHz Cortex A73 + Quad 2GHz Cortex A53 CPUs) with ARM Mali-G72 MP3 GPU
  * 6GB RAM, 64GB internal memory, expandable memory up to 256GB with microSD
  * Dual SIM (nano + nano + microSD)
  * ColorOS 5.2 based on Android 8.1 (Oreo)
  * 16MP rear camera with LED flash, f/1.85 aperture, secondary 2MP camera
  * 25MP front-facing camera, f/2.0 aperture, AI, Sony IMX576 sensor
  * Fingerprint sensor
  * 3.5mm audio jack
  * Dimensions: 156.7x74x7.9mm; Weight: 169g
  * Dual 4G VoLTE, WiFi 802.11 ac (2.4GHz + 5GHz), Bluetooth 4.2, GPS
  * 3500mAh (typical) / 3415mAh (minimum) battery with VOOC Flash charge

The OPPO F9 Pro comes in Sunrise Red, Twilight Blue and Starry Purple color, is priced at Rs. 23,990 and will be available for order through Flipkart, Amazon.in and offline stores starting today and will go on sale from August 31st through online and offline stores from August 31st.


Launch offers

  * Up to 3.2TB additional 4G data with Rs. 299 plan and benefits up to Rs. 4900
  * One-time display replacement
  * 3D metallic bottle for offline pre-orders
  * Up to 10% cashback (Maximum Rs. 2000) on ICIC Bank credit card EMI transactions for offline pre-orders
