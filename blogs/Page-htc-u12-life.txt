BUY

Dual camera, dual finish to shine

A dual camera that captures eye-catching photos and videos. A remarkable dual finish that offers a stunningly fresh take on design. A big battery that keeps up with your life. HTC U12 life is the phone where creativity meets beauty and stamina.

MAIN CAMERA


Artful bokeh

The exceptional dual camera takes the difficulty out of capturing artistic bokeh photos. Add next-level creativity to your photos with HTC U12 life.

Be the star you are destined to be

Create your next viral boom with flagship-level video recording. Shoot beautiful 4K videos with amazing clarity details. With all the details of 4K, your hi-res videos will be powerful and influential.

FRONT CAMERA


Selfies that shine through

A selfie cam with accurate flash and beautification mode gives you great looking selfies in low light that you'll be happy to show off to your friends.

DISPLAY


18:9 full body display

We believe enjoying your favorite content - movies, videos, games, whatever - shouldn't be compromised. That's why we're giving you an 18:9 screen with a full body view. The incredible contrast and vivid display will keep you captivated no matter what you're viewing.

DESIGN


Dual finish design shows its true stripes

A unique dual finish featuring soft, subtle stripes on an acrylic glass unibody with a metal like design offers a totally fresh take on our liquid surface inspired design. Enjoy a new dimensional touch with a solid grip that feels great in the hand. HTC U12 life is toughened up to fight against scratches to help keep your phone looking beautiful.

PERFORMANCE


Power packed

With such a gorgeous display and download speeds of up to 600mbs(i), you'll want to surf, play, watch and a whole lot more, all to your heart's content. The big 3600 mAh battery keeps up with whatever you want to do. And the Qualcomm® Snapdragon™ 636 mobile platform provides intelligent, chart-topping efficiency so you can multitask away without draining your battery.
