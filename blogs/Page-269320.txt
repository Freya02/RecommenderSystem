Honor 8C Unboxing and First Impressions



Huawei’s Honor brand is all set to launch the Honor 8C, successor of the Honor 7C in India next week. Here we have the unboxing of the smartphone.


Box Contents


  * Honor 8C smartphone in Aurora Blue color

  * 2-pin charger (5V-2A)
  * Micro USB Cable
  * SIM Ejector tool
  * Clear protective case
  * Quick start guide and warranty information

The Honor 8C has a large 6.26-inch HD+ 19:9 aspect ratio display with a notch and 1520 × 720 pixel resolution. Powered by Snapdragon 632 SoC with 4GB of RAM, it runs Android 8.1 (Oreo) with EMUI 8.2. Inside the notch there is an 8-megapixel camera that offers AI beautify features, earpiece and the usual set of sensors.


It has a chin below the display with Honor branding.


On the back there is a 13-megapixel primary rear camera with f/1.8 aperture and a secondary 2-megapixel camera with f/2.4 aperture for capturing depth information in portrait shots. It also has AI scene recognition to recognize over 500 scenarios in 22 categories.


The phone has an eye-catching 3D nano-level texture design that combines the gradient color and glare, offering a unique light and shadow circulation effect. There is also a fingerprint sensor on the back. It has Dual 4G VoLTE support, dedicated Dual SIM and microSD slots, and packs a 4000mAh battery.


Even though the phone has a polycarbonate body, the phone looks and feels premium. We should know the price of the phone in India when it is launched on November 29th, exclusively on Amazon.in. Complete review of the phone will be up soon.
